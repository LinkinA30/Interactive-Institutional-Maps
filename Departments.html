DEPARTMENTS

Computer Department

The department of Computer Engineering was established in 2008 with intake capacity of 60 students. Computer Engineering department will strive to achieve excellence in education so as to enable students to establish themselves as world-class technicians. The department will provide vibrant infrastructure and Computer tool sets to empower them with the proficiency and knowledge required to excel in the dynamic field of Computer and to pursue higher education.Computer Engineering department is committed to create and disseminate knowledge through innovative teaching & learning to provide world class technicians at diploma level.
